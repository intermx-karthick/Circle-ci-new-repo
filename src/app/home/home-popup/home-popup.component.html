As we continue building and improving the features of your product, we want your help!

As you explore the Geopath Insights Suite, you may realize that you have some questions about the software, the data itself, or usability. You might have some ideas for improvement, or come up with features that will make your job easier and more enjoyable.

To collect your thoughts, feedback, and ideas, we’ve placed a purple Feedback button at the bottom of every screen.

What does the purple Feedback button do?

By using this purple Feedback button (located on the bottom of every screen), you have the ability to ask questions directly, such as “What is an impression?” or “How do I add a user?”

If you run into any issues, bugs, or barriers to insight, those queries will automatically be sent to our support desk! From there, our support team and client mentors (yes, real humans!) are standing by to resolve your issues.

Not only will you be able to report bugs and ask questions, you can even submit new ideas for product features directly through this button. Simply type “I have an idea!” or “Idea” and you’ll be invited to share your idea with our team.

Automagically, your ideas arrive with our strategy division to vote on, prioritize, and deliver on your vision with upcoming product updates.
